## 9.1 Product File Packaging

### Core Playbook Files (7 Sections)
Combine all core sections into a single PDF or offer as individual downloads. Recommended: one combined document plus individual section PDFs as a bonus for navigability.

1. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-01-Foundation.pdf` — Budget, vision, and engagement foundation
2. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-02-Venue-Vendors.pdf` — Venue search and vendor booking system
3. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-03-Guest-Invitations.pdf` — Guest list, invitations, and website
4. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-04-Ceremony-Reception.pdf` — Ceremony planning and reception design
5. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-05-Countdown.pdf` — 6-month through 30-day timeline
6. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-06-Wedding-Day.pdf` — Day-of execution and logistics
7. `WeddingForge-2026-Section-07-After.pdf` — Post-wedding administration

**Combined file:** `WeddingForge-2026-Complete-Guide.pdf`

### Bonus Files (4 Bonuses)
1. `WeddingForge-2026-Bonus-01-Budget-Tracker.xlsx` — Google Sheets budget tracker (download as .xlsx for universal compatibility)
2. `WeddingForge-2026-Bonus-02-Notion-Dashboard.pdf` — Notion setup guide with template link
3. `WeddingForge-2026-Bonus-03-Video-Scripts.pdf` — 5 video scripts for social content
4. `WeddingForge-2026-Bonus-04-Checklists.pdf` — 25 checklists + Day-of Emergency Kit

### File Naming Conventions
- Use hyphens, not spaces or underscores
- Always include the year: `2026`
- Use title case for readability
- Format: `WeddingForge-2026-[Type]-[Number]-[Short-Title].pdf`

### PDF Export Settings
- **Page size:** US Letter (8.5 x 11 inches) — not A4, for North American printing compatibility
- **Margins:** 0.75 inch all sides minimum; 1 inch preferred for readability
- **Fonts:** Embed all fonts in the PDF — do not rely on system fonts
- **Body font:** Georgia, Garamond, or Lora (serif) at 11–12pt
- **Heading font:** Montserrat, Inter, or DM Sans (sans-serif) at 14–22pt depending on level
- **Compression:** Medium compression — balance between file size and image quality; target under 25MB per file
- **Color profile:** RGB for screen, CMYK only if print-on-demand is planned
- **Security:** Do not password-protect files (adds friction for buyers); watermarking is optional

### Cover Page Requirements
Every PDF needs a cover page with:
- Product title
- Subtitle
- WeddingForge branding
- Year (2026)
- Page count or section title
- Color-matched to the main cover design

### Table of Contents
- Generate a hyperlinked TOC as page 2 of the combined guide
- Use bookmarks/outline panel in the PDF for navigable sections
- Tools that produce clean TOC automatically: Notion export, Pandoc with custom CSS, or manual setup in Google Docs

### Combining Markdown Files Into a Single PDF — Tool Options

**Option 1: Pandoc (free, command line)**
```
pandoc section-01.md section-02.md ... -o WeddingForge-2026-Complete-Guide.pdf --pdf-engine=wkhtmltopdf --css=style.css
```
Best for: technical users who want precise control over typography and layout.

**Option 2: Markdown to PDF via Typora or Obsidian**
Open all files, apply a custom CSS theme, export. Fast and produces clean results with minimal setup.

**Option 3: Google Docs**
Paste all markdown content into a Google Doc, apply heading styles, generate a linked TOC, export as PDF. Most accessible option for non-technical creators.

**Option 4: Canva**
Use Canva's document editor to build a designed version. Import markdown content section by section, apply the brand style, export as PDF. Best for highest visual quality; most time-intensive.

**Recommended workflow:** Finalize content in Google Docs → export as PDF → add cover page in Canva → merge PDFs using Adobe Acrobat, Smallpdf, or PDF24.

## 9.2 Cover Design Brief

### Required Cover Elements
- **Title:** Wedding Planning & Execution Survival Guide 2026
- **Subtitle:** The Complete System for Staying Sane, On Budget, and Actually Present on Your Wedding Day
- **Author/Brand:** WeddingForge
- **Year badge or callout:** 2026 Edition

### Visual Direction
Warm, modern, and intentionally non-cheesy. The reference point is Architectural Digest meets a wedding planner's field notebook — elevated but practical. Think less blush-and-gold Pinterest wedding board, more confident grown-up who has done this before.

Avoid: stock photo couples, excessive florals, script fonts that sacrifice legibility, generic ring imagery.

Aim for: texture, restraint, confidence. A cover that looks credible on a professional's desk.

### Color Palette (choose one direction)
- **Direction A — Organic Warmth:** Cream (#F5F0E8), warm sage green (#8B9E7A), soft terracotta (#C47C5A)
- **Direction B — Modern Neutral:** Warm white (#FAFAF7), warm navy (#2C3E5D), dusty rose (#C9A49A)
- **Direction C — Minimal Editorial:** Off-white (#F7F4EF), warm charcoal (#3D3530), single accent in muted gold (#C4A95A)

### Typography
- **Title:** Clean serif — Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or EB Garamond at large scale
- **Subtitle:** Sans-serif — Montserrat Light or DM Sans Regular
- **Brand name / year:** Small caps or uppercase tracking — Montserrat Medium

## 9.4 Lead Magnet Funnel Setup

### Where to Host the Lead Magnet
- **Gumroad free download** (simplest): Set price to $0, require email, deliver the 12-page checklist PDF. Gumroad collects the email and you can export the list.
- **ConvertKit** (recommended for email growth): Create a landing page, deliver via automation. Integrates with most email platforms.
- **Beehiiv** (recommended if building a newsletter): Built-in landing pages, subscriber management, and analytics. Free plan supports lead magnets.

### 5-Email Welcome Sequence

**Email 1 — Delivered immediately upon signup**
- Subject line options:
  - "Your free wedding checklist is inside"
  - "Here's the checklist — and one thing to do right now"
  - "You asked for the checklist. Here it is."
- Content: Deliver the lead magnet download link. Brief warm welcome — 2–3 sentences. Tell them what's coming in the next few emails and why it's worth reading.
- CTA: Download the checklist and do one item on it today.

**Email 2 — Day 2**
- Subject line options:
  - "The one thing most couples forget in Month 1"
  - "This mistake costs couples $3,000+ (and it happens in Week 1)"
  - "Before you book anything, read this"
- Content: The budget category most couples underestimate (catering fees and service charges that aren't in the per-person quote). Teach something real and specific — not generic advice.
- CTA: Download or revisit the Budget Setup Checklist from the lead magnet.

**Email 3 — Day 4**
- Subject line options:
  - "How we cut $8,000 from a $35K wedding (real story)"
  - "Real numbers from a real wedding budget"
  - "The vendor they almost didn't negotiate with (and saved $4,200)"
- Content: A case study (real or composite) of a couple who used the framework to bring their budget from $38K down to $29K. Specific line items: florist substitutions, day-of coordinator instead of full planner, off-peak Friday date. Make it concrete and replicable.
- CTA: "I wrote the full version of this system into the Survival Guide — here's what's in it." Soft mention of the paid product with the Gumroad link.

**Email 4 — Day 6**
- Subject line options:
  - "Your vendor contract has a dangerous gap — here's what to add"
  - "The clause most wedding contracts don't include (but should)"
  - "Before you sign anything, check for this"
- Content: The force majeure and backup vendor clause — what it is, why most contracts don't have it, and the exact language to request. This email delivers genuine legal-adjacent value.
- CTA: "The full vendor contract review framework is in Section 2 of the guide." Include the Gumroad link with discount code SURVIVAL10.

**Email 5 — Day 8**
- Subject line options:
  - "The full guide is waiting for you (+ a discount code inside)"
  - "10% off ends soon — your code is SURVIVAL10"
  - "One more thing before I stop asking"
- Content: Soft pitch. Summarize what the full guide contains. Remind them of the problem it solves. Acknowledge that they've already gotten real value from the free emails — the guide is everything else. Include social proof if available (testimonials, download count, reviews).
- CTA: "Use SURVIVAL10 at checkout for 10% off." Direct Gumroad link. Tell them the code expires in 48 hours (creates urgency without being manipulative — set the code to actually expire).

## 9.5 Distribution Channels

### Pinterest
- **Board strategy:** Create a board titled "Wedding Planning Tips 2026" and a second titled "Wedding Budget & Vendor Advice." Pin to both.
- **Pin types that convert:** vertical text-based pins (1000x1500px) with a clear headline, one key tip in the body, and your URL. Not photos — text graphics.
- **Keyword targeting:** Use Pinterest's search bar to identify high-volume keywords: "wedding planning checklist," "wedding budget tips," "wedding vendor questions," "wedding timeline template." Include these in pin titles, descriptions, and board names.
- **Cadence:** 5–10 pins per week minimum for the first 30 days. Pinterest is a slow-burn channel — results compound over 60–90 days.

### Instagram and TikTok
- Pull content angles directly from the video scripts in Bonus 3
- Best-performing formats: "3 things your florist won't tell you," "the real cost of a wedding in 2026," "watch me build a wedding budget from scratch"
- Link in bio to the Gumroad listing or lead magnet landing page
- Reels and TikTok videos between 30–90 seconds perform best for this content category

### Reddit (value-first, never promotional)
- Target communities: r/weddingplanning, r/weddingsunder10k, r/frugalwedding, r/Weddings
- Rules for engagement: answer real questions with real detail. Do not post promotional content. Do not link to your product in comments unless directly asked and you disclose the affiliation.
- Strategy: become a recognizable helpful voice over 2–4 weeks. Then, in your profile bio, link to the lead magnet. Let people discover it.
- If you post original content (a checklist, a budget breakdown, a vendor Q&A), credit the guide naturally if asked — "I put this together for a guide I wrote, happy to share more if helpful."

### Facebook Groups
- Same approach as Reddit: answer questions helpfully and specifically, not promotionally
- Target groups with 10,000+ members in the wedding planning space
- Do not post promotional content directly — most groups prohibit it

### Etsy
- List the PDF guide as a digital download on Etsy
- Etsy title: "Wedding Planning Guide 2026 | Complete Planner PDF | Budget Tracker + 25 Checklists"
- Tags: use all 13 available tags with keyword variations
- Price: match your Gumroad price or offer at $52 (Etsy takes a fee, so adjust accordingly)
- Include 5 preview images — the same set used on Gumroad

### Email to Personal Network
Send this email (or a version of it) to your personal contacts on launch day:

> Subject: I made something and would love your eyes on it
>
> Hey [name],
>
> I've spent the last several weeks building a wedding planning guide I genuinely believe would have saved me [hours / money / stress] if I'd had it. It's live today.
>
> If you're planning a wedding, know someone who is, or work in the wedding industry — I'd love your support, your feedback, or just a share. Here's the link: [URL]
>

## 9.6 Pricing Strategy

### Launch Phase (First 30 Days)
- **Price:** $47
- **Messaging:** "Launch price — going up to $57 on [date]"
- Creates genuine urgency without manufactured scarcity. The price will actually go up.

### Evergreen Price (Day 31 Forward)
- **Price:** $57
- This is the anchor price for all future marketing and comparison framing ("less than one vendor meeting")

### Bundle Option
- **Guide + 1:1 Planning Consult (60-minute call):** $97
- Offered via a separate Gumroad listing or a Calendly booking link
- Best positioned for couples who are early in planning and want a fast-start session

### Discount Code for Lead Magnet Subscribers
- **Code:** SURVIVAL10
- **Value:** 15% off the evergreen price ($57 → $48.45)
- Communicate in Email 4 and Email 5 of the welcome sequence
- Set the code to expire 7 days after it's issued per subscriber (ConvertKit and Beehiiv can manage this)

### Refund Policy
**Recommended:** 30-day no-questions-asked refund.

Rationale: Digital product refund anxiety is real and directly reduces conversion rates. A stated 30-day guarantee removes the final hesitation for uncertain buyers. Actual refund rates on digital products are typically under 3%. The revenue gained from reduced hesitation outweighs refund losses at this price point.

State it clearly on the product page: "If this guide doesn't help you feel more organized, confident, and in control of your wedding planning within 30 days, email us for a full refund. No questions. No forms."
